About Me

I am a UX/UI Designer with a background in applied psychology. I am a problem solver, critical thinker and passionate idealist who strives to marry form and function in the digital space.

I acquired a wide range of skills throughout my education. I specialise in data analysis, user research and insight development, informed by the unique background in psychology. I am passionate about comprehending user behaviours, solving user problems and creating dynamic user interfaces to bring joy to the user experience.

My designs are always user-centred while remaining cognisant of business goals. I am a self-motivated learner and thrives on absorbing new information and skills.

About the Project


Jade Agencies is a Melbourne-based marketing agency that provides effective, research-driven strategies for Australian businesses looking to enter the Chinese market. The company aims to bridge Australia and China through small businesses. The current website design fails to convey the brand personality and communicate a sense of professionalism to users, which would affect the chance of customers choosing the business over other competitors existing in the industry. The solution was to renew the website so that it would showcase a combination of sleek modernity and Chinese cultural understanding while clearly providing all business information.


Role & Responsibilities: This is an individual project and I have been involved in all phases of the project by practising the design thinking process.


Duration: 3 weeks


Methods: User Interview, Persona Creation, Wireframes, Prototyping


Tools:Adobe XD, Photoshop, Miro, Zoom

RESEARCH

Usability testing - current site


I conducted 5 usability testson the challenges and opportunities of the current Jade Agencies website covering key navigation elements, page flow and layouts.


The key findings derived from the usability tests:


  • - Many participants were suggesting although the website adopted a clean and minimalist style, the design was too simple and plain which fails to communicate a good sense of brand personality and differentiate from its competitors in the field.
  • - Participants did not feel like the website was accommodating for a business audience since there was too little information on the website about their past work, business partners and clients.
  • - Some user interface design issues communicated the wrong messages to participants. Such as the underlined texts look like links that can be clicked.

Competitor Analysis

A competitor analysis was conducted to compare with both direct competitors and indirect competitors. Key competitive advantages of each organisation’s website were addressed below.

How might we make the current website design more appealing and engaging so that the Jade agency can attract more customers over its competitors?

TESTING

Usability Testing


I created a test plan for users to complete consecutive tasks on the high-fidelity prototype.


  • - 5 users, 4 tasks.
  • - 40% success rate for the ‘Read more on the financial service company’s case study task’.
  • - 100% success rate for the rest of tasks.

I gained the following insights:

  • - Three out of five participants struggled to find the ‘Show More’ tab to expand the paragraph on the case study page.
  • - One participant commented the chosen imagery could be improved to find images more fitting to the scenario.
  • - The stakeholder preferred the original layout of the information on our team page.
